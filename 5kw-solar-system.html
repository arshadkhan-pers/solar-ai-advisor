Frequently Asked Questions

How much electricity can a 5kW solar system generate?

Generation depends on location, weather and installation quality. Actual output varies.

How much roof space is required?

Most installations require approximately 400–500 square feet of shadow-free roof area.

Can batteries be added later?

Many systems can be upgraded later with battery storage, subject to technical compatibility.

Is subsidy guaranteed?

No. Subsidies are subject to government policies, eligibility criteria and approval processes.

Essential Knowledge

Performance in Low Light?

Modern photovoltaic cells are designed to capture diffuse sunlight, ensuring energy production even on overcast days.

What about storage?

Most urban installations use grid-tied systems. Excess energy is sent to the grid, and you receive credits via net metering.

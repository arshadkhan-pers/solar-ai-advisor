Frequently Asked Questions

How much electricity can a 1kW solar system generate?

A well-designed 1kW solar system typically generates approximately 1,400–1,600 units annually depending on location and weather conditions.

How much roof space is required?

Most installations require approximately 80–120 square feet of shadow-free roof area.

Can batteries be added later?

Many systems can be upgraded later with battery storage depending on inverter compatibility.

Is subsidy guaranteed?

No. Subsidies are subject to government policies, eligibility criteria and approval processes.

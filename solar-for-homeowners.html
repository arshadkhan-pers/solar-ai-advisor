Is Your Home Suitable For Solar?

Many homes can benefit from rooftop solar, but suitability depends on several factors.

  • ✓ Good sunlight exposure
  • ✓ Sufficient electricity consumption

  • ✓ Grid connection availability

Frequently Asked Questions

How much roof space is needed for solar?

Required roof area depends on system size, panel efficiency and installation layout.

Can solar work during cloudy weather?

Yes. Solar panels continue generating electricity during cloudy conditions, although production may be lower.

Do I need batteries?

Many residential solar systems operate without batteries. The best solution depends on your goals and local conditions.

How long do solar panels last?

Modern solar panels are designed for long operational lifetimes and often carry performance warranties extending decades.

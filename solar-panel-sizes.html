Solar Panel Sizes & Roof Space Guide

Understand solar panel dimensions, roof space requirements and how many panels you may need for your home.

What Is The Size Of A Solar Panel?

Most modern residential solar panels installed in India are approximately 1.7 to 2.3 metres long and 1.0 to 1.3 metres wide, depending on manufacturer, technology and wattage.

Typical Residential Solar Panel

  • ✓ Length: 1.7m – 2.3m
  • ✓ Width: 1.0m – 1.3m
  • ✓ Area: Approximately 20–30 sq ft
  • ✓ Power Output: 450W – 600W+

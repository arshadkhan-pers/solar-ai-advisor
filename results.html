Common Questions

Will solar work in clouds? ▼

Yes, panels utilize both direct and diffuse sunlight. While output drops on cloudy days, they continue to generate power.

What is Net Metering? ▼

It's a billing mechanism that credits solar energy owners for the electricity they add to the grid.

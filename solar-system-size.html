What Size Solar System Do I Need?

A complete guide to choosing the right solar system size for your home in India.

How Solar System Size Is Calculated

The easiest way to estimate solar size is by looking at your monthly electricity bill. Higher bills usually indicate higher electricity consumption and require larger systems.

Monthly Bill Recommended System
₹500 – ₹1,500 1–2 kW
₹1,500 – ₹3,000 2–3 kW
₹3,000 – ₹5,000 3–5 kW
₹5,000 – ₹10,000 5–8 kW
₹10,000+ 10 kW+

Popular Solar System Sizes

1 kW Solar System

Suitable for small homes with basic appliances. Typically generates 4–5 units per day.

2 kW Solar System

Suitable for small families. Can power lights, fans, television and refrigerator.

3 kW Solar System

One of the most popular residential sizes in India. Suitable for moderate electricity consumption.

5 kW Solar System

Ideal for households running air conditioners and multiple appliances. Can significantly reduce electricity bills.

10 kW Solar System

Designed for large homes and high-energy users. Provides substantial long-term savings.

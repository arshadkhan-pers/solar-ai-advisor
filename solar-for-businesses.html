Commercial Solar Financing

Many businesses explore financing options to reduce upfront investment requirements. Depending on the lender and project structure, financing may allow businesses to spread costs over time while benefiting from solar-generated electricity.

Financing terms, eligibility requirements and repayment structures vary between institutions and should be reviewed carefully before proceeding.

Net Metering For Businesses

Businesses operating during daylight hours may consume a large portion of the electricity generated by their solar system directly.

When eligible and approved, surplus electricity may be exported to the utility grid through net metering arrangements, subject to applicable regulations and utility policies.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can businesses install rooftop solar?

Many commercial properties can install rooftop solar, subject to technical and regulatory requirements.

Is solar suitable for small businesses?

Suitability depends on electricity consumption, roof availability and financial objectives.

Can solar improve business profitability?

How long do commercial solar systems last?

Commercial solar systems are typically designed for long operating lifetimes and often include extensive manufacturer warranties.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much electricity can a 4kW solar system generate?

A well-designed 4kW solar system typically generates approximately 5,600–6,400 units annually depending on location and weather conditions.

How much roof space is required?

Most installations require approximately 320–400 square feet of shadow-free roof area.

Can batteries be added later?

Many systems can be upgraded later with battery storage depending on inverter compatibility.

Is subsidy guaranteed?

No. Subsidies are subject to government policies, eligibility criteria and approval processes.

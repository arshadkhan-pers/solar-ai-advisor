## What Makes a Good Solar Panel?

**Efficiency**
Panel efficiency (typically 20–23% for modern mono panels) determines how much electricity is generated per square metre of roof. Higher efficiency is especially valuable when roof space is limited.

**Warranty terms**
Look for two warranties:
- *Product warranty* (10–12 years minimum) — covers manufacturing defects and physical failures
- *Performance warranty* (25 years) — guarantees the panel produces at least 80% of rated output after 25 years

**Manufacturer reputation and longevity**
Solar panels are meant to last 25+ years. A manufacturer that goes out of business in year 10 cannot honour warranty claims. Established brands with proven track records offer more reliable long-term security.

**After-sales support in India**
For imported brands, confirm whether the Indian distributor provides warranty support locally or whether claims must be routed through the manufacturer's home country. Local support matters when you have an issue.

## Monocrystalline vs Polycrystalline Panels

| Feature | Monocrystalline | Polycrystalline |
|---------|-----------------|-----------------|
| Efficiency | Higher (20–23%) | Lower (16–18%) |
| Appearance | Black cells, uniform | Blue cells, speckled |
| Performance in limited space | Better | Requires more roof area |
| Cost | Marginally higher | Slightly lower |
| Market trend | Dominant choice today | Less common in new installations |

**The practical conclusion:** For most Indian residential installations in 2026, monocrystalline (specifically PERC or TOPCon technology) is the default recommendation. The efficiency advantage means fewer panels for the same system size, which is valuable on typical Indian residential rooftops.

## How Panel Choice Fits Into the Overall Decision

Panel brand is one factor — but not the only one. The quality of the inverter, mounting structure, wiring, and the installer's workmanship matter equally to long-term system performance.

When evaluating proposals from multiple installers, ask specifically about:
- Panel make, model, and datasheet efficiency
- ALMM listing status (for subsidy)
- Inverter brand and warranty
- Mounting structure material (hot-dip galvanised or aluminium — not plain MS)

Frequently Asked Questions

Will solar work in cloudy weather?

Yes, modern panels generate power even on cloudy days, though output reduces slightly.

Do I need batteries?

Not required for grid-tied systems. You can use the grid as your battery through net metering.

Is the subsidy guaranteed?

Subsidies are available as per current government norms and subject to approval processes.

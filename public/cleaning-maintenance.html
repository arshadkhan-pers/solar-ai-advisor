Solar Panel Cleaning & Maintenance Guide

Learn how to keep your solar system operating efficiently and maximize long-term savings.

Do Solar Panels Need Cleaning?

Yes. Dust, bird droppings, leaves and pollution can reduce solar panel performance over time. Regular cleaning helps maintain energy production and system efficiency.

☀️

Typical Performance Impact

In dusty regions, dirty panels may experience noticeable reductions in energy production. Regular maintenance helps ensure optimal performance.

Recommended Cleaning Schedule

🌧️

Low Dust Areas

Every 4–6 months

🏡

Typical Residential Areas

Every 2–3 months

🏭

High Dust / Industrial Areas

Monthly inspections recommended

Safe Cleaning Tips

✓ Use clean water whenever possible
✓ Use a soft cloth or non-abrasive brush
✓ Clean during early morning or evening
✓ Follow manufacturer recommendations
✓ Consider professional services for difficult-to-access rooftops

Maintenance Checklist

✓ Visual inspection of panels
✓ Check for cracks or damage
✓ Monitor inverter status
✓ Review energy production
✓ Inspect wiring visibility
✓ Verify mounting structure condition

Solar AI Advisor Recommendation

Regular monitoring is often more important than cleaning itself. Homeowners should periodically review system generation and investigate significant performance changes.

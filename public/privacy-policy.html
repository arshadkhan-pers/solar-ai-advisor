Solar AI Advisor is an independent solar advisory platform that helps residential and commercial consumers evaluate rooftop solar opportunities, estimate savings, understand subsidies, compare options and connect with suitable installation partners.

By using our website, calculators, reports or services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

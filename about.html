Why We Built Solar AI Advisor

Buying rooftop solar should be simple. Unfortunately, many homeowners receive conflicting quotes, confusing subsidy information, and aggressive sales pitches.

Solar AI Advisor was created to bring transparency to the solar buying process. Our platform helps homeowners understand:

  • Recommended solar system size
  • Estimated installation cost
  • Government subsidy eligibility
  • Expected savings and payback
  • Installer readiness and buyer protection guidance

Our goal is simple: help Indian homeowners go solar with confidence.
